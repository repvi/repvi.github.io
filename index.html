What I Do

RTOS & Kernel Development

Built custom real-time operating systems from scratch for ARM Cortex-M processors. Expert in preemptive multitasking, priority-based scheduling, task management, and synchronization primitives (semaphores, mutexes, event flags).

Deep understanding of low-level systems programming, interrupt handling, context switching, and memory management with predictable real-time performance. Experience with FreeRTOS and custom RTOS implementations.

Embedded Communication

Design and implement custom communication protocols for embedded systems. Specialized in UART, ESP-NOW mesh networking, and binary protocol design achieving ultra-low latency (<1ms) and high reliability (99.9%+).

Developed production-ready mesh networks supporting 20+ devices with multi-hop routing, secure handshakes, and peer management. Experience with WiFi, MQTT, and IoT platform integration.

Firmware Architecture

Build scalable, maintainable firmware with enterprise-level architecture. Expert in driver development, hardware abstraction layers, and optimizing for performance, power consumption, and memory footprint.

Experience across STM32, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, and Arduino platforms. Proficient in C/C++, assembly optimization, OTA updates, and production-ready embedded systems with comprehensive error handling and testing.
