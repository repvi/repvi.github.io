About Me

I'm an embedded systems engineer with deep expertise in ESP32 firmware development and IoT solutions. My passion lies in bridging the gap between hardware and software, creating intelligent, efficient, and reliable embedded systems that solve real-world problems.

With extensive experience in microcontroller programming, sensor integration, wireless communication protocols, and real-time operating systems, I specialize in developing robust firmware that powers the next generation of connected devices. My work spans from low-level hardware drivers to complex application logic, always with a focus on performance, reliability, and maintainability.

I believe in open-source collaboration and continuous learning. You can find many of my projects on GitHub, where I share code, documentation, and insights from my embedded systems journey.
